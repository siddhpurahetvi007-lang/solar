About Drasti Solar Power

Your honest partner in India's transition to clean, affordable solar energy.

Our Story

Drasti Solar Power was born from a simple realization: Indian households and small businesses are paying too much for electricity, while abundant, free energy shines down on our rooftops every day.

As a growing solar startup founded by Jayesh Siddhpura, we realized that many people were deeply confused by complex solar quotes, hidden costs, and the frustrating process of claiming government subsidies. We decided to build a solar installation company that puts total transparency and customer education first. We believe that accessing clean energy and government solar subsidies in India shouldn't be a struggle.

Our Mission

To provide affordable rooftop solar systems to Indian homes and businesses by eliminating hidden fees and ensuring a smooth, fully supported installation experience from day one.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted partner for sustainable living, empowering communities to reduce their carbon footprint and achieve complete energy independence.
