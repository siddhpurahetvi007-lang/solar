Everything you need to know before installing a rooftop solar system in India.

The total cost depends on your daily electricity usage and the KW capacity required. However, thanks to a government solar subsidy in India like PM Surya Ghar, the upfront cost of residential solar solutions has dropped significantly, making affordable solar panels accessible to most households.

As a registered vendor, Drasti Solar Power handles the entire subsidy process on your behalf. We guide you through the PM Surya Ghar registration, coordinate the installation, and ensure the subsidy amount is successfully credited to your bank account.

High-quality solar panels used in our installations are incredibly durable and typically last 25 to 30 years without any drop in efficiency. The solar inverters usually require replacement only once every 10 to 15 years.

Yes! With proper system design and net metering approval from your utility company, a residential rooftop solar installation can wipe out your electricity bills completely, leaving only minimal fixed grid-connection charges.

The physical installation of the affordable solar panels typically takes just 1 to 3 days depending on the system size. However, government and net metering approvals can take a few weeks. We manage this entire timeline for you.

Rooftop solar systems require very low maintenance because they have no moving parts. Simply washing the panels with water a few times a month to remove dust is enough to keep them generating peak solar energy for homes.

Yes, solar panels still generate electricity even on cloudy days, though at a slightly reduced efficiency compared to direct sunlight. You will still achieve excellent solar energy savings year-round in India.
