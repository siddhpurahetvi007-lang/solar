Our Solar Installation Services

End-to-end solar energy solutions designed to fit your unique energy needs and budget.

Residential Rooftop Solar Installation

Say goodbye to unpredictable utility bills. Our residential solar solutions are tailored specifically for Indian households, transforming your empty roof into a silent, powerful energy generator.

We handle everything: site inspection, customized 3D design to maximize sun exposure, structural safety checks, and flawless installation of affordable solar panels. Enjoy decades of free, clean electricity right at home.

Solar Solutions for Small Businesses

High operational costs can cripple a growing business. Our commercial solar installation services help Indian startups, warehouses, and small enterprises stabilize their energy expenses.

Investing in a commercial rooftop solar system not only protects your bottom line from rising per-unit electricity costs, but also showcases your brand's commitment to corporate social responsibility and 100% clean energy.

Solar Consultation & System Design

Not sure where to start? We provide totally free, no-obligation solar consultations. We analyze your past electricity bills to understand your unique consumption patterns.

Then, our experts engineer a complete system design, recommending the exact KW capacity you need to achieve maximum solar energy savings without overspending on unnecessary equipment.

Government Solar Subsidy Assistance

The financial barrier to going solar is lower than ever thanks to state and central initiatives. As a registered vendor, we help our customers flawlessly navigate the PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana portal.

We process all necessary documentation, approvals, and inspections, ensuring you receive your eligible government solar subsidy directly to your bank account with zero hassle.

Solar Maintenance Services

While affordable solar panels are incredibly durable with no moving parts, routine maintenance ensures they generate peak power year-round.

Drasti Solar Power offers comprehensive cleaning, electrical inspection, and system monitoring services to keep your rooftop solar system operating flawlessly long after installation.
